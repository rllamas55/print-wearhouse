Sublimation

The process of printing onto a special sheet of paper, and then transferring that image onto fabric.

Silk Screen

The process of pulling a layer of ink over a screen to produce a design.

Vinyl Printing

Vinyl printing is all about heat transfer. A machine is used to cut out letters and designs from vinyl sheets and heat-press them onto the fabric.

Dark & Light Transfer

Heat transfer papers for both light and dark fabrics.

Embroidery
